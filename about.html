Mark Krupinsky

This page is about me, by me, and for you!

Childhood:

I was born in Baltimore, MD October 6, 1970 at University of Maryland Hospital. I grew up in Hampden MD, right outside of downtown Baltimore. I moved to Pennsylvania in 1983, and graduated High School in 1988.

Military Service:

I joined the US Army my Junior year of High School under the "delayed entry program". I graduated High School on June 6, 1988, and August 23, 1988 I began Basic Training at Ft. Leonardwood, MO. Upon completion of Basic, I went to Monterey, CA to attend the military's Language Schoolm at The Presidio of Monterey, otherwise know as DLI (Defense Language Institute). I have been to the US Army Airborne School at FT. Benning, GA, the US Army Air Assault School at Ft. Campbell, KY, and the US Army Military Intelligence Analysis School at GoodFellow Air Force BAse in San Angelo, TX. I am a veteran of Operation Desert Shield and Operation Desert Storm in 1990 - 1991. I ended my term of service in the US Army on February 14, 1997.

Currently:

I currently live in the GREATEST city on earth...New Orleans, Louisiana! Home of Mardi Gras! I am married, and have 2 sons, and 2 daughters.
